Frequently Answered Questions

Camp FAQs

What age groups are eligible to enroll?

Our clinics and camps are structured across multiple age brackets. Youth skills development accepts players from ages 9-13, while prospect camps and showcases require high school standing (ages 14-18).

Are parents allowed to observe sessions?

Yes. We encourage parents to attend our weekly recruitment seminar held during the Friday afternoon matches. Observing technical training is permitted from designated court observation boxes.

What is the coach-to-athlete ratio?

To guarantee personalized posture correction and metric coaching, we maintain a strict 1:8 ratio (one NCAA/certified coach for every eight players) across all camps.
