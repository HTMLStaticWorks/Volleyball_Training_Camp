Program Overview

Our High School Prospect Camp is built for competitive athletes seeking placement on college sports rosters. The training framework operates at high-tempo NCAA team speeds, challenging athletes' tactical awareness, physical positioning, and serving consistency.

During the 4-week program, players undergo vertical assessment, speed-ladder training, video evaluation of hand placements, and direct competitive match plays observed by collegiate coaches.

Daily Camp Schedule

08:30 AM - 09:30 AM

Warm-up & Plyometric Assessment

Dynamic stretching, core activation, and box jump metrics.

09:30 AM - 12:00 PM

Position-Specific Technical Drills

Setters work on posture; Hitters run tempo setups; Liberos practice dig redirection.

12:00 PM - 01:30 PM

Lunch & Classroom Tactical Video Huddle

Nutritional advice session followed by structural rotation pattern video reviews.

01:30 PM - 04:00 PM

6v6 Scrimmages & Metric Capturing

Competitive game states focused on tactical adjustments and block setups.
