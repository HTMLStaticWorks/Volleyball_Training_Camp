Biography

Marcus Sterling is a veteran front-end instructor and coach with over 18 years of high-performance volleyball experience. In his professional career, he competed in European leagues (Italy and Poland) as a primary Outside Hitter, securing three tournament MVP placements.

Transitioning into player development, Marcus served as Assistant Coach for the Ohio State men's volleyball team before taking the role of Technical Director at Spike Academy. He coordinates the college prospect showcase series and works directly with athletic directors to coordinate scholarship tryouts.

Training Philosophy

"We focus heavily on technical repetition and biomechanical precision. An extra inch on a vertical touch point or a split-second transition adjustment in cover setups can be the difference between a block out and a score. We build volleyball athletes with the mental endurance required for Division I sports."
